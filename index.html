Lottery Rules

To participate in the lottery, each user pays $20.

The lottery operates with the following rules:

  • Every user must follow the main Telegram channel to participate.
  • Each user can increase their chances in the lottery by inviting friends. For each invited person who participates, you get an additional chance in the lottery.
  • If one of your invited friends wins, you receive a $1000 reward, but you stay in the lottery unless you win the grand prize ($7000).
  • If a user wins the grand prize of $7000, all their chances will be removed from the lottery, but they can still receive rewards for their friends’ wins.
  • Every week, 10 participants will be randomly selected to win $7000 each.
  • The lottery starts when 2 million people participate, and the total prize pool will be distributed among winners.
  • If you invite multiple people, and one of your chances wins, all remaining chances are void, and you exit the lottery.

Please ensure you accept the rules before proceeding.
